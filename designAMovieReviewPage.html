Movie Review: Rise Beyond

Rise Beyond is a visually striking indie drama starring Ethan Carter as Kai Moreno, a shy high schooler who secretly dreams of performing on stage. After discovering an old guitar backstage at his school theater, Kai sets out to conquer stage fright, rediscover his late brother’s musical legacy, and shine under city lights.

Movie Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐☆☆☆ (7.8/10)
